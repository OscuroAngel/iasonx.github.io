About Me

Iason Xexakis is a pianist and composer of neoclassical / classical / meditative piano compositions.

He is also a music producer/ beatmaker, singer/ songwriter and an MC. The composer loves to experiment with other instruments such as the guitar, the drums and the drum pads.

Iason has studied music at the conservatorium of Nakas in Athens, at a young age, for a few years. His father influenced him greatly with greek traditional music, with sounds from the bouzouki and passed his artistic sensitivity to him.

For the past year, Iason, creates intensively sensitive melodies with soul captivating characteristics and has great aspirations to become as great as possible. His first single 'Pain' is a tribute to his father who passed away on January the 5th of the year 2020.

Iason is a fully independent artist. He records his music at his own studio.

Solace

Music can be often our Solace when we were having a hard time or when in stress. This piano solo by Iason is composed with a purpose to relax the mind and open the heart. Press play and let all your worries behind.

Pain

Iason's debut single is a piece of music that encapsulates the emotional state of the composer in one of his most difficult and life changing times of his life. It is an emotional waterfall that needed to be released for the first time ever, allowing to his musical creativity to be unfolded. A need of the soul, to express its testimony of the pain that endured during one of the most difficult and transformative times of Iason's life. A Break ups, the loss of friends and loss of his beloved father pushed Iason to chose the music path and release the 'Pain' he was experiencing, in an effort to heal his heart. This is dedicated to Kostas Xexakis, Iason's father, who heard the piece at the hospital, during his last days of his life.
